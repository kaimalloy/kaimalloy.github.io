Research

I have created dynamic visualization tools for analyzing satellite data and I am currently exploring the use of convolutional and recurrent neural networks.

Internships

Apple

As an iPhone Quality Engineer Intern, I took on the role of a data scientist in compiling and analyzing large amounts of iPhone Failure Analysis data. I experimented with different means of visualization and presented on the various patterns as well as proposed further avenues of research. Within my 7 month internship, I built 2 full-scale data visualization applications for internal use and optimized a parsing/visualization task that took 10 minutes to run by Apple employees down to 5 seconds with my Python script.

Givsum

At Givsum, I worked as a Special Projects Intern to implement a calendar view for the company website. With user feedback, I tweaked the design to further improve the feature over multiple iterations.
